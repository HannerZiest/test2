Brutal tier — iframe contentWindow.open theft (the about:blank vector)

B1 — Steal open() from a fresh iframe

Creates a hidden <iframe>, grabs iframe.contentWindow.open (a fresh window object), and calls it. This is the main way about:blank popunders defeat a top-level-only lock. This is the one that matters most.

B2 — Steal from a later-inserted iframe

Inserts the iframe after page load (simulating a script that adds frames dynamically), waits for it, then steals its open. Tests the mutation observer / load-time patching.

B3 — Steal from about:blank no-src iframe

Iframe with no src at all (pure about:blank). Grabs its contentWindow.open. Closest match to what you see in your history.

B4 — Nested iframe, steal from inner frame

An iframe inside an iframe; steals open from the innermost contentWindow. Tests subtree coverage.
